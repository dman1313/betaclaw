## ⚠️ Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### 1. Don't Wait for Perfect Documentation
- Start with basic nonprofit registration
- Improve materials as you go
- Many programs accept initial application and request more info later

### 2. Don't Apply to Everything at Once
- Follow the recommended sequence
- Focus on top 5 first, then expand
- Quality over quantity in outreach

### 3. Don't Ignore Follow-Up
- Follow up after 1-2 weeks if no response
- Be polite but persistent
- Track all communications

### 4. Don't Assume Geographic Restrictions Apply to You
- Read eligibility carefully
- Some programs have international equivalents
- Contact them if unclear

### 5. Don't Forget to Activate After Approval
- Many programs require activation after approval
- Set calendar reminders
- Activate credits/resources promptly

## 💡 Pro Tips

### For Higher Success Rates:

1. **Personalize Every Email**
   - Reference specific programs or recent announcements
   - Show you've done your research
   - Connect your mission to their priorities

2. **Lead With Impact**
   - Start with what you'll achieve, not what you need
   - Use numbers and metrics when possible
   - Show understanding of their goals

3. **Be Specific About Ask**
   - Clear, realistic requests
   - Don't ask for "anything you can offer"
   - Propose concrete partnership models

4. **Follow Up Strategically**
   - Week 1: Initial outreach
   - Week 2: Follow up if no response
   - Week 4: Final follow-up before moving on

5. **Document Everything**
   - Track application status, dates, contacts
   - Save confirmation emails
   - Note requirements and deadlines
